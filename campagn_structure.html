Meta Video Ad Testing Strategy

High-Converting Formats

  • The "Green-to-Clean" Timelapse: Highly satisfying visual transformations of dirty pools becoming crystal clear. Excellent for stopping the scroll.
  • Technician Walkthrough: Showcasing uniformed techs, branded trucks, and professional LSI chemical balancing (The "Anti-Splash & Dash").
  • Equipment Spotlight: Behind-the-scenes look at Hayward/Pentair installs for high-ticket repair targeting.

Hooks & Script Angles

  • Hook 1 (Pain Point): "Tired of pool guys who just splash and dash? Here is how a true professional..."
  • Hook 2 (Authority): "Why luxury pools in California trust our specific LSI balancing method..."
  • Hook 3 (Urgency): "Pool heater making this noise? We are your certified warranty station."

Meta Best Practices

  • Format: 9:16 (Reels/Stories) & 4:5 (Feed).
  • Duration: 10-15s for top-of-funnel, up to 30s for Retargeting.
  • Accessibility: Heavy bold text overlays & subtitles (most users watch without sound).
  • CTA: "$149 Diagnostic Visit" persistently on screen.
